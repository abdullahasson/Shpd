Everything You Need for Shipping, All in One Platform

Shpd helps you streamline orders, shipments, and courier accounts from a single dashboard, so you can spend less time managing logistics and more time growing your business.

1

All Orders in One Dashboard

No more switching between platforms! Shpd centralizes all your orders—from online stores, social media, and manual entries—into one view.

Orders from Shopify, WooCommerce, Salla, and more sync automatically to your dashboard.

Generate custom shipping links to capture orders from Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, WhatsApp, and keep them organized.

Easily add orders manually and integrate them smoothly into your workflow.

2

One Hub for All Your Couriers

Manage all your courier accounts in a single interface with Shpd.

Instantly connect with DHL, FedEx, Aramex, J&T, Bosta, and more.

Generate labels, track shipments, and manage couriers without leaving your dashboard.

Choose the best courier for each shipment by comparing rates and services.

3

Effortless Order Management

Take control of your fulfillment process with Shpd’s easy-to-use order management.

Quickly review, confirm, and mark orders as ready to ship.

Handle returns just as smoothly as new orders from the same dashboard.

Simplify everything from order creation to fulfillment in one organized space.

4

Flexible Shipping Options

Shpd provides the shipping flexibility your business needs.

Access exclusive rates with trusted courier partners.

Prefer to use your own couriers? Connect your accounts directly to Shpd.

Whether you’re shipping locally or internationally, choose what works best for you.

5

Centralized & Real-Time Tracking

Stay updated on every shipment with real-time tracking.

See the status of every package, no matter the courier.

Keep customers informed with real-time tracking information sent directly to them.

6

Simplified COD Reports

Easily manage Cash on Delivery (COD) payments with Shpd’s comprehensive reporting tools.

View accurate reports on outstanding and completed payments.

Monitor COD across all couriers and shipments from one dashboard.

7

Team Access for Seamless Collaboration

Work better together by adding team members to your Shpd account.

Invite your team to manage orders and shipments with you.

Control access to sensitive information and assign permissions.

Process orders quickly and efficiently as a team.

8

Reliable Customer Support, Always Here for You

Get help when you need it with Shpd’s dedicated support team.

Reach out to our team through chat for quick answers.

Send us an email for in-depth assistance.

We’re here to ensure you have everything you need to keep your shipping smooth.
