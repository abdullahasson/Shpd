About Shpd

Royal Works, the proud owner of the "Shpd" brand, is a leading force in the logistics industry, providing innovative solutions tailored to meet the ever-changing needs of our clients. With registrations in both Egypt and France, we showcase our steadfast commitment to quality and reliability in every service we offer.

The Vision Behind Shpd

"Shpd" represents our vision of delivering comprehensive logistics services that cater to a wide array of markets. We are dedicated to maintaining transparency and efficiency in our operations, ensuring that our partners enjoy an exceptional experience.

At Shpd, our customers come first. We are committed to delivering:
Exceptional Shipping Experiences

We aim for excellence at every stage of the shipping process.

Comprehensive Shipment Tracking

From the moment of booking to the final delivery, our customers stay informed every step of the way.

Prompt Response to Feedback

We actively welcome and address customer complaints and suggestions, fostering continuous improvement and growth.

Strong Collaboration with Shipping Partners

We maintain close relationships with our contracted shipping companies to ensure timely and accurate service.

Thorough Staff Training and Development

Our team is equipped to provide quick and effective answers to all customer inquiries.

As we look ahead, we remain committed to expanding our services and delivering the best logistical solutions in the industry.
