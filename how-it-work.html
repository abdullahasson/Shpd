Shipping Made Simple in Just 3 Steps

Designed to be easy, so you can focus on what you do best—growing your business.

Streamline Your Shipping with Shpd:

1

Connect Your Store or Social Media

  • How It Works: Sign up free and integrate your favorite sales channels like Shopify, WooCommerce, or Salla for automatic order syncing.
  • No Store? No Problem: Create a custom shipping link to share on social media or messaging apps like Instagram, Facebook, and WhatsApp. Customers can easily enter their details, which automatically appear in your dashboard.
  • Why This Helps: With everything in one view, you’ll save time and avoid manual data entry errors.
2

Confirm Orders & Manage Status

  • How It Works: Review orders in your dashboard and confirm items that are ready to ship. Track orders through a simple pipeline—unfulfilled, confirmed, shipped, or canceled. Keep it organized and efficient as you process each order.
  • Why You’ll Love It: Streamlined order management means fewer mistakes and happier customers. You’ll stay on top of every order, big or small.
3

Choose Your Courier & Ship

  • How It Works: Select a courier based on your preferences. Use discounted shipping rates or connect your own accounts. Create shipments, print labels, and track packages from a single dashboard.
  • Why It Matters: Optimize your shipping costs and speed by choosing the right courier for each order. Real-time tracking ensures you and your customers stay informed every step of the way.
